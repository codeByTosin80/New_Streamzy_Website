What is STREAMZY?

Streamzy is a movie and TV show website where you can enjoy different kinds of videos anytime you want. With Streamzy, you don't need to go to the cinema, you can stay at home or anywhere and watch movies on your phone, laptop, or TV. You can watch action films, love stories, cartoons, drama, and many more. Its made for everyone, both young and old. All you need to do is sign up, choose a plan, and enjoy watching.

TV Shows & Movies

You can watch a variety of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more. The more you watch, the better STREAMZY gets at recommending TV shows and movies.

Supported Devices

You can watch STREAMZY on a variety of devices, including smart TVs, streaming media players, game consoles, set-top boxes, Blu-ray players, and mobile devices.

Plans and Pricing

Each STREAMZY plan determines the number of devices you can watch STREAMZY on at the same time and whether you can watch in High Definition (HD), Full High Definition (FHD), or Ultra High Definition (UHD). You can change your plan at any time.

Get Started

To start watching STREAMZY:

  1. Create an account by entering your name, email address and creating a password.
  2. Choose a plan.

As a STREAMZY member, you are charged once a month on the date you signed up.
